Project Name: BTC-USD Price Prediction

Description

BBitcoin, the pioneering cryptocurrency, has gained immense popularity and market significance. However, the volatility and unpredictability of its price pose challenges for investors seeking to optimize their investment strategies. This project aims to address this challenge by leveraging historical Bitcoin price data and employing Machine Learning techniques to predict future price trends.

Requirements

  • Python
  • Numpy
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Scikit-Learn
